Go Africa processes the data collected for the creation and personalization of your account, to allow us to manage your contact, quote, purchase and investment requests.

Data marked by an asterisk (*) must be provided in registration.

If you are a project owner, your data will be communicated exclusively to the Go Africa's technical partners in charge of the study of the projects. If you are an investor or a supplier then your data will not be communicated to third party providers..

The data collected will be kept for a period of 5 years from the closing of the account

At any moment you can :
  • Access your data
  • Edit your personnal informations
  • Delete your data by providing a request
  • Exercise your right to limit the processing of your data

To exercise these rights or for any questions about the processing of your data in this system, you can contact our department in charge of exercising these rights at the following address: contact@goafrica.io.
